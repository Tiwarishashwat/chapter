Working

What we do?

Its the small things that make us different

Tech-Workshops

Workshops should be an integral part of our academic curriculum, as they not only provide us with abundant ledge on the subject in question, but also teach us the importance of team work, group discussions and sharing experiences. They teach us something outside the theoretical boundaries of our books. We at ACE, keeping academic excellence as a goal in mind, conduct regular intensive workshops for our students.

Projects

Projects on various technological advancements and upcoming new technologies is a major focus in ACE student chapter.We provide a platform for students to embark on a journey of technological experimentation, leading to the birth of innovative ideas and successfully working models. Our future scope for the chapter is aimed at creating an environment rich in resources required for such growth.

Online Events

UPES ACE SC organises various online competitions ranging from technical and non-technical quizzes to fun events. Online Competitions not only help you in enhancing your skills but is also easy to participate in. Events that are held online reach large number of people and it turns out as a great way to showcase your potential talent to the world.
Instagram series: Terror 404!

Fun Events

When you become an adult, there’s no sudden, magic transformation that happens, telling you all you can do from here on out for fun is host wine and cheese parties.Similarly, when you’re a kid, it doesn’t all have to be about running around like a headless chicken (although that’s pretty fun).The truth is:Fun is fun. It’s universal.
At UPES ACE SC, we believe that life is to be enjoyed, not just endured and that is why we bring to you loads of fun events and competitions, where yo get to showcase your extra curricular skills.

About ACE

With our proud sponsor Microsoft Student Partner, we bring to you UPES ACE(Academic And Cocurricular Excellence) SC where we work not only to accomplish certain goals, but to have an allround development for everyone working with us. To tell a great story, you must have one and to have a great story, you need to get out of your comfort zone and start working. We at UPES ACE SC provide you a platform to showcase your skills  and helps you push your limits beyond your imagination. We believe having fun is an integral part of our lives and that is why at UPES ACE SC, we work hard and play harder. "We are not just a Student Chapter, we are a family away from our families."
